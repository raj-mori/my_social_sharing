  1. Website Information
    1. About The Website
      1. Name of Website: Webspasm
      2. Tagline: ‘Backbone of the Internet'
      3. Type: Webspasm is a mixture of a couple of websites. Reddit / Wikipedia / Pinterest / Delicious
        1. It’s similar to Reddit because of the URL structure and how every subject has it’s own section. For example reddit has ’subreddits’ based on different topics like games, movies, or hobbies. Each board will be it’s own ‘subreddit’ kind of.
        2. It’s similar to wikipedia - again because of the layout of each board page based on each subject.  It’s also similar because each board can be discussed about - although not edited publicly like wikipedia.
        3. Webspasm is the most similar to pinterest in the sense that users will be finding useful websites on the internet and categorizing them (bookmarking them) on boards (similar to ‘pinning’ them).
        4. It’s similar to delicious just because delicious is/was a good social bookmarking service.
      5. Use: The main aspect of webspasm is for users and administrators to collect high quality websites around the internet about specific categories and display information about them. Users will be able to login, create/remove boards, add/remove bookmarks about specific topics and discuss them.
    3. Terminology
      1. Board: A board will contain information about a subject. It will contain both panels and bookmarks. A board for example is about a specific subject such as ‘our solar system’, or ‘list of fruits’, or ’varieties of apples'. The board will have a bunch of images (bookmarks) on panels (white box) to display them.
      2. Panel: A panel will be the box that holds the bookmarks. There will be multiple panels on a board page.

      3. Bookmark: A bookmark will be the 200x50 image that links to either an outside website, board, or a user. Bookmarks also may be referenced as ‘strips’ as thats what we used to call them.
        1. There are also 3 different types of strips or bookmarks. The board strip is a 200x50 image and has a black bar going at the bottom. A user strip that has a black bar in the middle. And a normal strip which will have text in different directions. You can see the differences of these on the index page.
      5. Hover: When a user hovers over specific text on the website a ‘hover’ will appear. These hovers will show information about that specific text. Those texts will be boards, users, and bookmarks. This will work very similar to kongregate.com’s hover system when you hover over a game name.
      6. Modal: A modal is a popup that comes up over the current web page when a user clicks on a specific link or button.
